Our Features

The service we offer is specifically designed to meet your needs.

CI/CD Foundations (Starter)

  • Pipeline-as-Code: Build/test/deploy with GitHub Actions/Jenkins/Harness, reusable templates and approvals.
  • Automated Testing & Releases: Unit/integration, artifacts & SBOMs, semantic versioning, auto-changelogs.
  • Environment Promotion: Dev → Stage → Prod with gated rollouts and rollback runbooks.

Kubernetes & GitOps Platform

  • Hardened Clusters: EKS/GKE/AKS baselines, autoscaling, network policies.
  • GitOps Delivery: Harness GitOps (Argo under the hood) + Helm; canary/blue-green + Feature Flags.
  • Rancher Fleet (multi-cluster GitOps) + Helm/Kustomize; progressive via Flagger/SMI.

DevSecOps & Supply-Chain Security

  • Shift-Left Scanning: SAST/DAST, secrets and dependency scans on every PR.
  • Policy & Provenance: OPA/Kyverno gates, container/image signing with cosign (SLSA-ready).
  • Secrets & Access: Vault/Secrets Manager with short-lived OIDC credentials.

Observability & SRE Essentials

  • Unified Telemetry: Metrics/Logs/Traces via Prometheus, Loki, Tempo, Grafana dashboards.
  • SLOs & Incident Response: Error budgets, alert routing, runbooks, postmortem templates.
  • Reliability Hygiene: Alert noise reduction, MTTR tracking, health checks per service.

QA Automation & Quality Gates

  • Test Strategy: Unit/integration/e2e + contract testing (Pact) wired into pipelines with flaky-test quarantine.
  • Quality Signals: Coverage thresholds, mutation testing, SonarQube/Semgrep gates, SBOM validation before release.
  • Test Data & Environments: Ephemeral test stacks, seeded datasets, deterministic builds for reproducible results.

FinOps Lite (Cost & Efficiency)

  • Quick Wins: Rightsizing, autoscaling, lifecycle/retention policies built into pipelines.
  • Spend Visibility: Budgets, anomaly alerts, per-service cost dashboards.
  • Commit Savings: Savings Plans/Committed Use analysis and monthly optimization playbook.
